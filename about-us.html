Who Are We ?

Salsarte
Dancing School

We Are The BEST Dancing School in Dubai
We offer our customers the best quality and service as entertainment and dance,
More than just learning steps,
You will learn to LOVE dancing, and be an Excellent dancer.

Salsarte Dancing School provides the opportunity for everyone to learn from the best dancers in the world, in a wide range of styles and techniques.
Our vision is to create magical moments which will stay in the hearts of our students, we aim to help all the dancers to evolve and develop their techniques to reach a proficiency that will be recognized in the dancing community .. not only in Dubai .. but around the world

We Offer many dance styles for all age groups and have dedicated classes for adults and children in all forms of dance.
Classes are available in Salsa, Bachata, Kizomba, Hip Hop and more

Our Instructors are the best in Dubai ( and the world ) and have a long experience in teaching and performing around the world
